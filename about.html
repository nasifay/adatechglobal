About Adatech Global

Adatech (Ada Technology) is an Ethiopian innovation-driven tech company bridging the gap between hardware and software. We build integrated digital solutions that empower startups, businesses, and communities to thrive in a connected future.

Hardware Innovation

From IoT devices to embedded systems, we design and build smart hardware solutions including smart beds, smart blankets, automation systems, and custom electronics.

Software Solutions

Scalable applications—from enterprise systems to mobile apps—built to optimize operations, increase productivity, and accelerate digital transformation.

IoT & Automation

We integrate sensors, connectivity, and intelligent automation to create smart environments and future-ready solutions.

Innovation for Africa

Rooted in Ethiopian ingenuity, we design solutions tailored to African realities—scalable, inclusive, affordable, and sustainable.
